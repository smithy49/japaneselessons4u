About

My name is Sonoko and I am a native Japanese woman who has been living in the UK since 1996.
I teach Japanese Language and Culture to people of any age and level, from beginners to advanced.
I gained a qualification in Teaching Japanese as a Foreign Language from the Institute of International Education in London (IIEL).
The Institute of International Education in London (IIEL) is an international educational institution based in London.
