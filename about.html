About

I am a native Japanese woman who has been living in the UK since 1996.
I teach Japanese Language and Culture to people of any age and level, from beginners to advanced.
I gained a qualification in Teaching Japanese as a Foreign Language from the Institute of International Education in London (IIEL).

The Institute of International Education in London (IIEL) is an international educational institution based in London.
In addition to research into educational issues, the IIEL offers several courses such as MA in Language Learning and Japanese Language Teaching, Postgraduate Diploma / Certificate in Teaching Japanese as a Foreign Language, Japanese as a Foreign Language (JFL) and the British Council accredited English as a Second Language (ESL).
