Welcome to Sonoko's Japanese Lessons - SJL

Are you thinking of improving your speaking skills or simply want to take it up as your new hobby?

Learn Japanese from the comfort of your own home. Lessons are flexible and you can learn at your own pace.

No prior knowledge of Japanese is necessary. All you'll need is a keen interest to learn.

If you just need a crash course in the language, such as if you intend to visit Japan, then this can be arranged.

Lessons are given online via Microsoft Teams | Zoom on a one-to-one basis.

Currently I live in Reading, Berkshire, and I am a qualified and experienced Japanese Language Tutor. The list below should help you judge what suits you best.

  • Lessons from beginner to advanced.
  • All ages welcome.
  • Lessons tailored to your requirements.
  • Learn at your own pace.

Some of the textbooks used for teaching are Minna no Nihongo, Genki and Japanese for Busy People.
